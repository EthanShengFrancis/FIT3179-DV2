Top 6 Countries Ranking Bump Chart

Singapore, Indonesia, Brunei, and India all maintain their rankings during 2019 and 2024. However, Singapore and China had a drop during 2020 and 2021 due to Covid 19. India, Brunei, and Thailand roughly had an increase during Covid but decrease later on in the years.

GDP vs Weekly Seats per Flight

The bubble chart shows that flights and seats increased across all regions. The results can be correlated with the rise in GDP in 2022, 2021 GDP decreased significantly due to Covid 19 restrictions.

GDP Growth Rate (2019-2024)

Covid 19 affected Malaysia's economy negatively in 2020-2021, and slowly recovered in 2022-2024.

Weekly Flights and Seats for different regions (2022-2024)

The overall trend of the chart increases through out 2022 to 2024. The increase in flights would contribute to the rise in GDP after covid restrictions were lifted.

Monthly Arrival Line Chart (2019-2024)

2019 held a high standard of visitors through the months compared to 2020 until 2024. There was a consistent positive difference after 2024 May when the visitor rates were the highest out of all the years, reaching its peak on 2024 December.

Domestic and Foreign Tourists per States (2024)

Kuala Lumpur has the highest foreigner count and has a larger difference compared to all the other states. Domestic tourist from richer states like Kuala Lumpur and Selangor prefer a nearby state like pahang for vacation. On the other hand, the other states would go to Kuala Lumpur which is the main state.
